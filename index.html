About QRT Coin

QRT Coin is a reward platform that distributes its native token (QRT) to users who engage with short-form video advertisements and partner applications. Our mission is to create a fair reward economy that compensates users for their attention.

How It Works

  1. Sign up with your email
  2. Watch up to 10 ads per day
  3. Install partner apps to earn bonus QRT (max 3/day)
  4. Push rewards to your QRT wallet and withdraw via Binance P2P or Payoneer
